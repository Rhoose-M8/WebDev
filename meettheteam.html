An All-Star Crew

Josh, Our Leader:

Josh embarked on this roofing journey just two years ago but has already made a significant mark in the industry. Beyond his impressive roofing skills, Josh is known for his excellent sense of humor, which adds a touch of laughter to every workday. He's not just about jokes, though; Josh boasts a world-class work ethic that inspires the entire team. His presence on the work site is nothing short of awesome, ensuring that every project is handled with precision and care. With Josh at the helm, Premium Roofing NZ has become a trusted name in the Hawke's Bay area for top-notch roofing solutions.

Outside of work, Josh is an adventurous spirit. When he's not overseeing roofing projects, you'll find him exploring the rugged landscapes of New Zealand, often on a hiking trail or kayaking down a picturesque river. Josh's dedication to his work and his zest for life make him a remarkable leader in the roofing industry.

Steve, Our Number 2 Man

Steve joined forces with Josh's team 18 months ago, bringing with him a wealth of experience and a reputation for hard work. Beyond his impressive roofing skills, Steve is the go-to guy for a good laugh on the job site. He's known for organizing team-building events that strengthen the camaraderie among the Premium Roofing NZ crew, fostering a tight-knit and productive workforce.
