Roofing, in Review

Our Original Logo

One sunny afternoon, shortly after registering the company, Josh sat down with a vision in his mind. With unwavering determination and a knack for design, he sketched out the company's original logo. It was a moment of inspiration, where creativity met entrepreneurship.

The logo, a testament to his dedication, represented not only a roofing business but a dream taking flight. It captured the essence of Premium Roofing NZ, setting the tone for the quality and craftsmanship that would define the company for years to come.

Our First Truck

In the early days of Premium Roofing NZ, the company faced the exciting challenge of acquiring its first truck. Thanks to an exceptionally generous loan from an aunt, the dream became a reality. They scoured second-hand car dealerships until they found the perfect fit.

That truck wasn't just a vehicle; it symbolized growth and opportunity. It carried the team and their tools to countless job sites, fostering the company's reputation for reliability and dedication to customer service.

Josh's Apprenticeship

Josh's journey to becoming the leader of Premium Roofing NZ began with a transformative apprenticeship at "Complete Roofing" under the strict guidance of Darren Pollard. Darren was a disciplinarian who demanded nothing short of perfection. It was under his watchful eye that Josh honed his skills, learning the ropes of the roofing trade.

Darren's unwavering commitment to quality and precision became Josh's mantra. Today, Premium Roofing NZ stands as a testament to the exceptional quality instilled in Josh during his apprenticeship, delivering top-notch results to our valued customers.
